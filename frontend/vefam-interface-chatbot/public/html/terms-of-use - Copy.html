2. Definitions

In these Terms:
"Non-fungible token" "NFT": means Ethereum-based tokens complying with the ERC-721 standard, ERC-1155 standard or other similar “non-fungible” token standards (eg: VIP-181, VIP-210 on Vechain). NFTs are intended to be “non-fungible” tokens representing a unique Collectible; however, certain NFTs may be fungible with one another (i.e., all such NFTs are associated with the same Collectible Metadata) while being non-fungible with other NFTs (i.e., such NFTs are associated with different Collectible Metadata).
"Fungible items": can be exchanged because their value defines them rather than their unique properties. For example, BTC or dollars are fungible because 0.01 BTC / $1 USD is exchangeable for another 0.01 BTC / $1 USD.
